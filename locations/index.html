Location


 

You’re welcome in 238 every day from 11:00am until late. In the morning you can start your day with a nice coffee, but also for a fresh ecological lunch the 238 is the perfect spot. In the afternoon you can relax with a nice beer or glass of wine combined with some nice bites, or you can try 238’s tea with sweets. In the evening you can visit 238 for a full 8 course menu or only come by for a few snacks, 238 doors are open for you the whole day!

 

Opening hours:
Monday - Sunday
11:00-00:00 All day

 

238 Restaurant

238 is located on Hong Kong’s art and antique area on the Hollywood Road at the Sheung Wan district. Hollywood Road runs through central and Sheung Wan. 238 is located at the end of the street in Sheung Wan, near the corner with Queens Road West.
